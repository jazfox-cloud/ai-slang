What does GEO mean?

Generative Engine Optimization: SEO's anxious cousin for getting mentioned, cited, or represented inside AI-generated search answers.

GEO means optimizing content for AI answer engines, not only classic search rankings. The practical question is whether a generative search system can understand, trust, and cite your content.

Origin and usage

Formalized in research on generative engines, then picked up by publishers, marketers, and SEO teams trying to understand visibility when answers are synthesized instead of listed as blue links.

Source type: paper. Last checked: 2026-07-13.

Grounded in the Generative Engine Optimization research paper; industry usage is broader and sometimes more marketing-heavy than the original framework.
