What does Google Zero mean?

The nightmare scenario for publishers and SEO teams: Google answers the user directly, and the website that supplied the knowledge gets zero click.

Google Zero means search traffic falling toward nothing because AI search gives users the answer before they visit the original site.

Origin and usage

Popularized in media and tech commentary as AI Overviews and AI search pushed more answers onto the search results page itself.

Source type: community. Last checked: 2026-07-03.

Publisher and tech-media shorthand tied to AI search and zero-click behavior; useful as a community term, not a formal Google product name.
