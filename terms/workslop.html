Workslop Meaning

AI-generated workplace output that looks polished enough to pass along but lacks the judgment, context, or substance needed to actually move the work forward.

What does Workslop mean?

AI-generated workplace output that looks polished enough to pass along but lacks the judgment, context, or substance needed to actually move the work forward.

Workslop is sloppy AI work dressed up as finished work. It usually shifts the hard thinking, checking, or rewriting onto someone else.

Origin and usage

Popularized by a 2025 Harvard Business Review article from BetterUp and Stanford-linked researchers studying low-value AI output in the workplace.

Source type: community. Last checked: 2026-07-11.

Workplace AI slang with a stable HBR reference; the term is useful as a cultural label, not a precise productivity metric for every AI-assisted document.
