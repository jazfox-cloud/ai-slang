What does Shadow AI mean?

Unauthorized AI use inside a company: employees pasting work into tools, installing assistants, or wiring model APIs around policy because the approved stack is too slow.

Shadow AI is AI work happening outside official company controls. It can create privacy, security, compliance, and quality risks even when employees are only trying to move faster.

Origin and usage

Adapted from 'shadow IT' as generative AI tools spread through workplaces faster than security, legal, and procurement teams could govern them.

Source type: technical-term. Last checked: 2026-07-07.

Enterprise AI governance term with roots in shadow IT; IBM and other security vendors use it for unsanctioned AI tools and workflows.

Examples

  • The official policy banned shadow AI, but half the team was summarizing sales calls in random browser extensions.
  • Shadow AI is not always malicious; it is often a sign that the approved workflow is useless.
