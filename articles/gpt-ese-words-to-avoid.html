GPT-ese Words to Avoid

GPT-ese is not one forbidden word. It is a pattern: polished transitions, inflated metaphors, and corporate softness that makes writing feel generated even when the facts are fine.

Common tells

Words like delve, tapestry, moreover, furthermore, and game-changer are not always wrong. They become suspicious when they appear in clusters and replace specific thinking.

How to rewrite it

Cut the ceremonial opening, name the concrete claim, and replace soft metaphors with plain verbs. If a sentence sounds like it is wearing a blazer to say nothing, make it shorter.

Use the humanizer carefully

A humanizer should help you edit, not launder weak writing. The best result still needs a point of view, real examples, and facts you can defend.
